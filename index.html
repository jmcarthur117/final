BLACK ENVELOPE

Choose your own adventure. Through the use of the nonlinear structure like Zork and an interface, I aimed to create a chose your own adventure game. My piece is inspired by what we have learned from the nonlinear narrative and interface stretches. The nonlinear interface focuses on storytelling in association with an interactive interface that focuses on user input as they navigate a story. Showing the potential stories have when you combine nonlinear narratives and interfaces.
